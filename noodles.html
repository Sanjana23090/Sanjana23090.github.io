Noodles

Description

Veg noodles aka Vegetable noodles is an easy to make indo-chinese dish that serves as a quick dinner option.
It's a complete meal and you won't need any side dishes..

Ingredients

(2 servings)

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 125gm noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic,ginger 1 inch
  • Chopped veggies
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1/4 tsp pepper powder

Instructions

  1. firstly, in a large vessel take 6 cup water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil. once the water comes to a boil place a pack of hakka noodles (125 gm)
  2. boil the noodles for 3 minutes or till it turns al dente. refer package instructions to know the exact cooking time.
  3. drain off the water from noodles and pour 1 cup of cold water to prevent from further cooking.
  4. drizzle 1 tsp oil and mix gently, to avoid noodles from sticking to each other. keep aside.
  5. now in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and 1 chilli. saute ½ onion, 2 tbsp spring onion on high flame. further add ½ carrot, ¼ green capsicum, 5 snow pea, 5 beans, chopped, ¼ red capsicum and 5 florets broccoli.
  6. stir fry on high flame for 2 minutes or until veggies are cooked yet retain their crunch. also add 3 tbsp cabbage and stir fry.
  7. further add 2 tbsp tomato sauce, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tbsp chilli sauce, ¼ tsp pepper powder and ½ tsp salt. stir fry until sauces are well combined. add in boiled noodles and mix gently.

Garnish with spring onion and enjoy veg noodles.
